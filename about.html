About Oyster Personal Finance

Empowering Financial Freedom in South Africa

Who We Are

Oyster Personal Finance is a trusted provider of personal and business loan solutions for South Africans. We are committed to simplifying credit access and supporting economic growth across the country.

With years of experience in the financial services industry, we understand the unique challenges faced by individuals and businesses in South Africa. Our innovative approach to lending combines cutting-edge technology with personalized service to deliver fast, reliable, and transparent financial solutions.

As a registered credit provider (NCRCP11356), we operate under the strict guidelines of the National Credit Regulator, ensuring that our practices are ethical, transparent, and in the best interests of our clients.

Our Mission

To provide quick, easy, and secure loans to individuals and small businesses through innovative technology, while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and financial responsibility.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most reliable and accessible micro-lending platform, empowering financial freedom and supporting economic growth in communities across the nation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I check my application status?

You can check your application status by visiting our Profile page and entering your email and PIN, or by calling our customer service line at +27 12 345 6789.

What documents do I need for a loan application?

You'll need a valid ID or passport, proof of income (payslips or bank statements), and proof of residence. Additional documents may be required depending on the loan type.

How long does the approval process take?

Our approval process typically takes 24-48 hours. Once approved, funds are usually transferred within the same business day or the next business day.

Can I apply for multiple loans at once?

We recommend completing one loan application at a time. Once your current loan is processed, you can apply for additional loans if needed.

About Oyster Finance

Oyster Personal Finance is a trusted provider of personal and business loan solutions for South Africans. We are committed to simplifying credit access and supporting economic growth.

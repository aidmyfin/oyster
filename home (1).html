Types of online loans we help you compare

Same day loans

Same day loans provide fast access to cash, often paid out within hours, making them ideal for emergencies or urgent bills.

Short-term loans

Short-term loans in South Africa are ideal for covering unexpected expenses, offering quick access to cash but usually with higher interest rates.

Instant loans

Instant loans are designed for speed, with online applications approved within minutes and funds transferred shortly after.

Cash loans

Cash loans provide quick access to money, often paid out directly into your bank account to help cover short-term financial gaps.

Personal loans

Personal loans offer flexible financing for various needs, from home improvements to debt consolidation, with fixed repayment terms and competitive interest rates.

Payday loans

Payday loans are short-term loans meant to tide you over until your next salary, offering quick cash for urgent expenses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How long does approval take?

Our approval process is typically completed within 24 hours. Once approved, funds are usually transferred to your account within the same business day or the next business day.

Is my information safe?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade security measures and encryption to protect your personal and financial information. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Do you charge hidden fees?

No. We believe in complete transparency. All fees and charges are disclosed upfront before you commit to the loan. There are no hidden costs or surprise fees.

What are the eligibility requirements?

To qualify for a loan, you must be at least 18 years old, a South African citizen or permanent resident, have a valid ID, and provide proof of steady income. Additional requirements may apply depending on the loan type.
